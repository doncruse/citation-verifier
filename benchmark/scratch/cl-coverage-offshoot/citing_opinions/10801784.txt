11        Noting that this case is still in the discovery phase, considering the general parameters of 
12   relevance and proportionality of Rule 26, which also apply to subpoenas served pursuant to Rule 
13   45, (see Fed. R. Civ. P. 45, 1970 Advisory Committee Notes (“[T]he scope of discovery through a 
14   subpoena is the same as that applicable to Rule 34 and the other discovery rules.”)), and keeping 
15   in mind the subpoenaed-party’s status as a third-party, the Court ORDERS as follows:
