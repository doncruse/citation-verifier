Morrison v. Walker - Summary for Brief Assessment

TOPICS FOUND:
- Repeated filings: "The record shows that he repeatedly made filings based only on the meritless falsified-perjury claim" (lines 270-271). Morgan filed an amended complaint (Jan 2014), second amended complaint (May 2014), and motion to dismiss (Aug 2015).
- Standard for 1927: "Conduct is 'unreasonable and vexatious' if there is evidence of the 'persistent prosecution of a meritless claim' and of a 'reckless disregard of the duty owed to the court.'" (lines 221-226)

TOPICS NOT FOUND:
- Does NOT use the phrase "dispositive rulings" or "clear record of delay"
- Does NOT characterize sanctions as an "extraordinary remedy"
- Does NOT state 1927 "must not be used to punish counsel for pursuing claims that ultimately fail"
- Uses "reckless disregard" standard, not "bad faith" as stated in the brief
