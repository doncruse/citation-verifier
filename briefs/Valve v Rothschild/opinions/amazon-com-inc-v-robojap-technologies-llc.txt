18          Plaintiff Amazon.com, Inc. (“Amazon”) provides the following status report regarding
19 service on Defendants Kumar Sachdeva (“Kumar”) and Gureen Pawar (“Pawar”). Amazon
20 incorporates by reference its prior status reports, Dkt. Nos. 117, 121, and 122.

22 and Pawar could not be located at their last known addresses, which Amazon provided in its

23 Hague Convention service documents. Since then, Amazon has made diligent efforts to identify

24 the whereabouts of Kumar and Pawar, and has continued its efforts to serve them.

25          Pursuant to the Court’s Order (Dkt. 119), Amazon will file another status report
26 regarding service on Defendants in 90 days or in the event that service is completed before then.
